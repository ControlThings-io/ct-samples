control4-sample.pcap

A sample packet capture from a Control4 appliance that includes OTA key
delivery to a downstream device.  You can extract the key with zbdsniff,
and decrypt the contents with Wireshark.  Remember to enter the key
backward in Wireshark to get it to decrypt properly.
